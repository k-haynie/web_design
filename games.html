My Favorite Games

The Beginner's Guide

Take a journey through the mind of an introspective game developer through his creations, and discover the impact of validation, security, and fulfillment.

Detroit: Become Human

In a futuristic earth, androids are critical to a functioning society. In this gorgeous game, see the world from the perspective of the androids - simultaneously adored and despised.

Outlast 2

Get trapped in your darkest nightmare, a cultic outpost with corpses and satanic imagery at every exhausting turn.

Presentable Liberty

What if prison was the safest place to be? In this indie game, try and survive your prison sentence as disease ravages the outside world.

Life is Strange

A teenager in a small town is suddenly bestowed with the power to rewind time. But her powers only go so far as space and time wreak havoc on those she loves.

Little Nightmares II

Try and escape an onslaught of bloated and lurching villains as a tiny child in a blue and glitchy hellscape.

Doki Doki Literature Club

Reading will not always keep you out of trouble. Hidden beneath the guise of an anime graphic novel, psychological horror awaits those who join the Literature Club.

Papers, Please

Take on the role of a border agent in Cold War Arstotzka, fending off desperate relatives, secret agents, and insurgents to decide the fate of your family.

Bendy and the Ink Machine

Old Disney cartoons always did have an odd, darker side to them. Composed of thick, black ink, Bendy and his cohorts are in pursuit of new prey.

Layers of Fear 1

An old, crippled artist has greater ailments then his leg. As he hobbles around his house, his paintings come alive - exploding in still-life apples and very real blood.
